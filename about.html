Our Story

With over 15 years of experience in the IT consulting industry, Unisonix LLC has established itself as a trusted partner for businesses seeking to leverage technology for competitive advantage. Founded in 2011, we've grown from a small team of passionate technologists to a full-service IT consulting firm serving clients across multiple industries.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to help businesses navigate the complex world of technology and unlock their digital potential. Today, we continue that mission with the same dedication and expertise that defined our founding principles.
